The Indian Statistical Institute is an institution of national repute, offering unique courses in Statistics, Economics, Computer Science, Cryptology & Security and SQC & OR. Founded by Professor P.C. Mahalanobis, the institute gained National Importance by an act of the Indian Parliament in 1959. Students are selected after a very rigorous entrance examination and interview. Our batch strength is relatively small to ensure the highest quality among students. Every year some of the best-reputed firms in the areas of Blockchain, Data Science, Technology, Finance, Economics, Analytics, Cyber Security, Consultancy, and Software Development recruit our students on campus. Companies who have recruited in the past have been consistently visiting our campus and foster a long-lasting relationship with us.

M.Tech in Cryptology and security is a special course offered by the institute. The course is designed to impart in-depth theoretical and practical knowledge in the area of cryptology and information security. It is designed to provide the basic background in mathematics, statistics and computer science followed by specialized instructions on various theoretical and practical aspects of the field.

To have significant impact in this field of research, the Centre actively acquires an eminent body of permanent faculty members, as well as hosts a continuous flux of esteemed visitors and students. PhD students at the Centre are full-time research fellows of Indian Statistical Institute. The full faculty information can be found here.
